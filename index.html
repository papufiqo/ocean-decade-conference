About the Conference

The 2026 Ocean Decade Conference in Barcelona will celebrate the progress made and set priorities for the remaining years of the UN Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainable Development (2021-2030).

Hosted by the Government of Spain and co-organized with the Intergovernmental Oceanographic Commission of UNESCO, this landmark event aims to generate the "science we need for the ocean we want".

The conference will focus on identifying science-based solutions for key challenges including climate change, biodiversity loss, pollution, food security, and building a sustainable ocean economy.
